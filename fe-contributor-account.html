Rebuilding Figure Eight Contributor Account

Design Thinking, UX/UI Design, User Testing

Project Objective

We wanted to rebuild Figure Eight’s contributor platform. The goal is to own the platform code and ship a light-weight, modern solution within a quarter.

I wanted to create an easy-to-use and engaging experience for users that work on our platform.

About Figure Eight

Data scientists and machine learning scientists use Figure Eight to clean data. The cleaned data are used to train machine learning algorithms. Data scientists or machine learning scientists set requirements on the final results. Contributors from around the world follow the requirements to clean data.

Understanding the Users

Our contributors are from around the world. English is often their second language. This is a flexible opportunity for them to earn extra income. Figure Eight Contributor Account is the main platform for them to find work.

Contributor Persona.

Sign Up Flow

In the old platform, sign up has been a main pain point for users. Users would often get confused by the process and drop out of the funnel.

The old elite sign up screen.
The main goal of the redesign is to create an easy sign-up flow and decrease the number of drop-offs.

The first screen of the sign up flow explain the value of the platform and answers concerns. To create an account, the first step only asks for required information. The design guides the user through each step of the sign up flow. It is allows users to skip to minimize drop offs. I added illustration and animation to create a delightful experience.

Finding Product-Market Fit with MVP

After our experimental success, we tied the product back to advocate marketing. Companies need a pipeline of new and original content for their lead-generation funnels. It is time-consuming and resource-intensive to maintain a consistent of stream of content. We wanted to create an efficient process to generate high-quality content at scale.

I wanted to create an effortless experience that streamlines the content creation process.

I worked closely with vendor and content-generators to iterate on the product. I also worked with a UI designer to bring the product to life.

After 4 months of working on the product, we were able to secure 7 SaaS companies to be paying customers.
